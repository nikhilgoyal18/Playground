### What top PMs figured out `Product`
- PM onboarding pattern: run Granola (AI meeting notes) in every stakeholder call, then prompt Claude after each one — "what did I just learn about where this product is struggling?" — one PM used this to write a strategy memo in 45 minutes instead of 4 hours
- The **meeting layer**: connect Granola transcripts to a Claude Project so they accumulate; a call from 3 weeks ago becomes context for today's pitch or stakeholder message
- The **research layer**: use Perplexity + NotebookLM in sequence — upload 6 months of customer interview transcripts and competitor teardowns to NotebookLM, then query across all ("what did churned enterprise customers say about onboarding?" returns cited answers from 40 interviews in seconds)
- Handoff pattern: run competitive research in Perplexity → save as markdown → feed into Claude as context; Perplexity finds what's happening now, Claude reasons about what it means for your specific product
- The **spec layer** shift: old sequence (idea → PRD → debate → build) → new sequence (idea → AI brainstorm → vibe-code prototype → team experiences it → refine → PRD → build)
